Short Story

Yellowstone National Park

One day my family took a trip down to Chico Hotspring. We decied that it would be a good place to celebrate the newyear.

Our first night we relaxed in the pools.

The following day we decied to go into Yellowstone. Everything was frosted over.

There was so much wildlife to be seen. We saw coyotes, eagles, elk, deer, and buffalo. Buffalo are my favorite.

They could be seen from either side of our car. Big clouds of steam filled the air as they breathed. The blue sky behind them became a stunning background.

After a long wonderful day we all headed back to our hotel tell room where we enjoyed our last night together as a family.
